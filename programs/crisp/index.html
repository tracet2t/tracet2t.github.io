Frequently Asked Questions

Is T2T a university program?
No. T2T is not a degree-awarding university program. It is an industry-readiness and skill-bridging program designed to support students and graduates.
Will I get a job after T2T?
T2T does not guarantee employment. However, it is designed to improve your employability by helping you build skills, confidence, exposure, and portfolio evidence.
Is this only for IT students?
CRISP Batch 4 is mainly suitable for IT, computing, software engineering, and related students. Students from related fields who want digital and industry-readiness skills may also apply.
Do I need to be technically strong?
You do not need to be an expert. But you should have a basic learning foundation and a willingness to improve.
What is the difference between Foundation and Practitioner?
Foundation is a 2-month career-readiness and industry-readiness program. Practitioner is a 6-month pathway that includes Foundation plus 4 months of mentor-driven industry project exposure.
Why is there a fee?
The fee supports program coordination, mentor involvement, workshops, learning activities, operations, assessment, and the guided experience provided to participants.
Is there a certificate?
Participants who complete the required activities may receive certification or recognition based on their completion level.
How do I apply?
Click the Apply for Batch 4 button or contact the T2T team via WhatsApp.
