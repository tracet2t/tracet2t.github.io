What is T2T AI-X?

This is not an academic course or a generic prompting camp. It is a high-performance career acceleration cohort designed for the AI-assisted software engineering era.

Phase 1

3-Month Intensive AI-Native Acceleration

A high-density operational phase covering the mechanics of AI-assisted engineering:

  • Interactive live workshops & technical coaching
  • Hands-on AI-assisted SDLC workflows
  • Systems thinking & architecture engineering
  • Building structural AI-augmented systems
  • Personal capability tracking & portfolio creation
Phase 2

Guided Industry-Aligned Execution

Selected cohort members transition into practical execution spaces:

  • Collaboration on live sandbox projects
  • Exposure to realistic engineering backlogs
  • Code reviews and structural audit sessions
  • Guided production releases & systems optimization
  • Advanced portfolio hardening for global readiness
